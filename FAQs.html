Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers about our sustainable bags.

What materials are used in your bags?

Our bags are made using authentic African wax print fabrics (Ankara), cotton lining, and eco-friendly reinforcements to ensure both sustainability and durability.

How do I care for my African print bag?

Spot clean with a damp cloth and mild soap. Do not machine wash. Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight to preserve the vibrancy of the fabric.

Do you offer custom orders?

Yes! You can place custom orders for bulk or personal use. Visit our “Order in Quantities” page to get started.

How long does it take to receive my order?

Standard orders are processed within 3–5 business days. Delivery time depends on your location and shipping option chosen.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship worldwide! Shipping costs and delivery times may vary depending on your country.

Can I return or exchange an item?

We accept returns or exchanges within 14 days of delivery, as long as the item is unused and in original condition.

How sustainable are your products?

All our bags are handcrafted using ethically sourced fabrics and minimal-waste techniques. Sustainability is a core value at Ama Oduma.
