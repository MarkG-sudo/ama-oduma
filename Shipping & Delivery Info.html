Shipping & Delivery

Everything you need to know about how we ship your sustainable styles

Delivery Areas

We deliver locally within Ghana and ship internationally to select regions. If you're unsure whether we ship to your location, please reach out via our Contact Page.

Processing Time

All bags are handmade and crafted with care. Standard processing time is 3–5 business days before shipping. For bulk or custom orders, processing time may vary.

Shipping Options

  • Local Delivery (within Ghana): 1–3 business days after dispatch
  • International Shipping: 5–14 business days (carrier and customs dependent)

Delivery Notes

Please ensure your contact and delivery information is accurate. Missed deliveries due to incorrect details may incur additional shipping fees.

Shipping Confirmation & Tracking

Once your order is shipped, you’ll receive an email confirmation along with tracking details (where available).

❗ Delays & Support

While we strive to meet estimated delivery windows, delays may occur due to holidays or carrier issues. If your order is delayed beyond the expected timeframe, kindly contact our team for support.
